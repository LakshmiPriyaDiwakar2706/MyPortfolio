About Me

Hey there! I'm Lakshmi Priya Diwakar, and I'm genuinely excited that you’re here to learn more about me. Let's dive into my story, shall we?

Picture this: I started off as an Engineer in Instrumentation Engineering, surrounded by circuits and industrial gadgets. Along the way, I stumbled upon programming languages like Python and Java. Trust me, back then, coding felt like trying to solve a Rubik’s cube blindfolded—utterly terrifying!

But then, the COVID pandemic hit, and I decided to give coding one more shot. Thanks to bite-sized YouTube tutorials (shoutout to those life-saving YouTubers!), I got the hang of basic C programming, Python, and databases. Miraculously, this led to a Software Engineer job at LTIMindtree. There, I was introduced to the mysterious world of Mainframe Systems and got to work with clients every single day.

Here’s where things got interesting. Each day, I felt like a superhero solving business problems. While others dreaded production issues, I embraced them like an old friend, determined not just to fix them but to make sure they never dared to return. I realized I had a knack for tackling tough challenges head-on.

One day, during my usual YouTube binge, a video on Analytics caught my eye. That was it—the spark I needed! Suddenly, I was diving into certifications and projects, all while dreaming of going back to school. And guess what? I made it happen! Now, I’m 8,698 miles away from home, soaking up knowledge in subjects I absolutely love at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

So, that’s my journey—filled with unexpected twists, a lot of learning, and a whole lot of fun. Thanks for tagging along!
